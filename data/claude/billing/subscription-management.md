# Managing your Claude subscription

You can manage your Claude Pro or Claude Max subscription from the
**Settings > Billing** page on claude.ai.

## Upgrading

Choose **Upgrade plan** and select the plan you want. The change takes
effect immediately and a pro-rated charge applies for the remainder of the
current billing period.

## Downgrading

You can downgrade at any time. The new, lower plan takes effect at the
start of the next billing period; you keep the higher plan's features
until then.

## Cancellation

Choose **Cancel subscription**. Your access continues until the end of the
current billing period. You will not be charged again unless you resubscribe.

## Refunds

Subscription refunds are evaluated case-by-case by the billing team. In
general, we issue refunds for:

- A double charge within the same billing period.
- An accidental upgrade reported within 24 hours.
- A clear service outage that prevented use of paid features.

To request a refund, contact support with the invoice ID. The billing team
will verify your identity before issuing any refund.
