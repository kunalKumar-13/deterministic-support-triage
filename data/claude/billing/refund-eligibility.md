# Claude subscription refund eligibility

Refunds for Claude Pro, Claude Team, and Claude Enterprise subscriptions
are evaluated case by case. As a general rule:

- **Accidental upgrade reported within 24 hours**: usually refundable.
- **Double-charge in the same billing period**: refundable; we will
  also block the duplicate at the card-network level.
- **Service outage that prevented use of paid features**: refundable
  pro-rated to the outage duration.
- **Cancellation just after a renewal you forgot about**: not
  automatically refundable, but the billing team can issue goodwill
  credit in some cases.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method and may take 5–10
business days to appear.

## Identity verification

Before a refund is issued, the billing team verifies the requesting
user's identity using the email of record plus a one-time passcode. We
do not process refunds based on agent commitments that we cannot
verify in our system.
