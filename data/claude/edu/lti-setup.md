# Setting up Claude for an LTI-based classroom

Claude offers LTI 1.3 integration so professors can provision Claude
access to students from inside a learning-management system (LMS) like
Canvas, Moodle, or Blackboard.

## To set up an LTI integration

1. Request LTI access from your Claude account team or via the
   Education contact form on claude.com.
2. Once approved, you'll receive an LTI **issuer**, **client ID**, and
   the JWKS/launch URLs to enter into your LMS.
3. In your LMS, add Claude as an external tool using those values.
4. Configure roster sync (recommended): students are then provisioned
   into a class-scoped Claude workspace automatically.

## Education pricing

Verified educational institutions qualify for discounted seats. Reach
out to the Education team with your institution's domain and student
count to get a quote.

## Privacy

LTI-managed student accounts have data-retention defaults aligned with
FERPA-style expectations: conversations are private to the student,
and instructors do not see student conversation content unless the
student explicitly shares it.
