# Deleting or renaming a Claude conversation

You can delete an individual Claude conversation if it contains
information you do not want kept on file.

## To delete a single conversation

1. Open the conversation you want to delete.
2. Click on the **name of the conversation** at the top of the screen.
3. From the menu that appears, select **Delete**.
4. Confirm the deletion.

## To rename a conversation

1. Open the conversation.
2. Click on the conversation name at the top.
3. Choose **Rename** and enter the new title.

## What happens to the underlying data

Deleting a conversation removes it from your account and queues it for
permanent deletion from Anthropic's storage according to the retention
schedule published on privacy.claude.com. Uploaded files attached to the
conversation are also deleted as part of this process.

If you used the conversation for sensitive content, also consider using
**Temporary Chat** going forward — these conversations are not saved to
your history.
