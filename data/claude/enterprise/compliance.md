# Claude enterprise compliance — HIPAA, BAA, data residency

Claude Enterprise is the right tier for organizations with
regulated-data requirements. Standard Claude consumer and Claude Team
subscriptions are not certified for HIPAA-regulated workloads.

## HIPAA

For HIPAA-regulated use cases, contact the Claude Enterprise sales team
to request a Business Associate Agreement (BAA). Once a BAA is signed,
you can use Claude within the agreed scope.

## Data residency

Enterprise customers can request that conversations and uploaded files
be stored in a specified region. Available regions and exact retention
windows are documented in your Enterprise contract.

## Sub-processors and security artifacts

Anthropic publishes its sub-processor list and current security
certifications (SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001) on the Trust Center. SOC 2
reports and DPA templates are available under NDA from your account
team.
