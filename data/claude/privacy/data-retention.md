# Claude data retention and deletion

Anthropic retains conversation data according to the published retention
schedule. You can manage your data through:

- **Delete a conversation**: removes a single chat from your history and
  schedules it for permanent deletion.
- **Temporary Chat**: starts a conversation that is never saved to your
  history.
- **Account deletion**: removes all your conversations and personal
  data, subject to legal-hold exceptions.

## Right to erasure (GDPR Article 17)

If you reside in the EU or UK, you can request deletion of your personal
data under GDPR Article 17. Send the request through the Privacy contact
form on privacy.claude.com. The standard response time is up to 30 days;
urgent requests can be flagged for expedited review.

Backups and operational logs may retain data for a short period after
deletion (typically 30 days) before being purged.

## Model training opt-out

You can opt your account out of model training in **Settings > Privacy
& data controls**. New conversations after the opt-out are not used to
improve models. Past conversations remain subject to the prior settings.
