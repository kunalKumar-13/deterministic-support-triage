# Conversation and workspace data isolation in Claude

Each Claude account has its own private conversation history. Other
users — including members of your workspace, family-shared Apple/Google
accounts, or other people who use the same browser profile after signing
out — cannot see your conversations.

## What is NOT shared

- Personal conversation history between Claude accounts.
- Uploaded files between separate accounts.
- Project instructions or memories between accounts.

## What IS shared (when you opt in)

- Conversations explicitly shared via the **Share** link feature.
- Files added to a Project you've invited collaborators to.
- Workspace-level resources in a Claude Team or Enterprise workspace.

## If you see someone else's data

If you believe another user's conversations are visible in your account,
treat this as a serious privacy concern: sign out immediately and
contact support. We will escalate to the trust-and-safety team for
investigation. Do not attempt to read or copy the content you're seeing.
