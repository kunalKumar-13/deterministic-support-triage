# Trouble signing in to Claude

If you cannot sign in to claude.ai, try the following steps in order.

1. Make sure you are using the email address you originally signed up with.
   If you signed up with Google, you must continue to use the **Continue
   with Google** option.
2. If you signed up with email + password, use **Forgot password?** to
   send a reset link. The link is delivered from `noreply@anthropic.com`
   and is valid for 60 minutes.
3. Clear your browser cookies for `claude.ai` and try again.
4. Try a private/incognito window to rule out a browser extension.

If you have a Claude account through your workplace (Teams or Enterprise),
your IT administrator may have configured single sign-on. In that case,
sign in through the identity provider link you were given when invited.
