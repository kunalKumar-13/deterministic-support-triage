# Reporting a lost or stolen Visa card

Always call the customer-service number on the back of your card first.
The card-issuing bank handles the block, replacement, and any related
disputes.

## Visa Global Customer Assistance Service (GCAS)

If you cannot reach the issuing bank, Visa GCAS is available 24/7 to
help block the card and arrange emergency assistance:

- **India toll-free**: 000-800-100-1219
- **Global (from anywhere)**: +1 303 967 1090

GCAS can typically block a reported card within about 30 minutes,
arrange emergency cash (subject to availability and identity
verification), and coordinate with the issuer to send a replacement
card.

## What you should do immediately

1. Call your issuing bank using the number on the back of your card.
2. If you cannot reach them, call the appropriate GCAS line above.
3. If the card was lost in a country where you are traveling, also file
   a police report — it is often required for fraudulent-charge claims.
4. Review recent transactions and flag any you do not recognize.
