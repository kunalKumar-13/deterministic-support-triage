# Replacing a lost or stolen Visa card

If your Visa card is lost or stolen, contact your issuing bank immediately.
Visa itself does not issue cards directly; your card is issued by your
bank or credit union, and the bank handles replacement.

Most banks can:

- Block the lost card to prevent further use.
- Issue a replacement card with a new number, typically within 5-10
  business days.
- Provide an emergency cash advance through a Visa Emergency Service if
  you are travelling.

If you suspect fraudulent transactions on the lost card, request that the
bank open a dispute case in addition to blocking the card. Keep the
reference number the bank provides; it is required for any follow-up.
