# Reporting Visa card fraud

If you believe your Visa card has been used fraudulently or your identity
has been compromised, act quickly:

1. **Block the card.** Call the customer-service number on the back of
   your card and ask the issuing bank to block the card immediately.
2. **Document the fraudulent charges.** Note dates, amounts, merchant
   names. The bank will need this when opening cases.
3. **Open a dispute** for each fraudulent charge. The bank typically
   issues a provisional credit while it investigates.
4. **File a police report** if local laws require one for fraud cases.
   Some banks need a police-report number to finalise the chargeback.
5. **Watch for follow-on attempts.** If the card was used for online
   purchases, enable transaction alerts and consider freezing your credit
   bureau file.

Visa never asks for your full card number, CVV, or one-time passcode by
unsolicited email or phone call. Treat any such request as a phishing
attempt.
