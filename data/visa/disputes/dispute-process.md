# Disputing a Visa transaction

If you see a charge on your statement that you do not recognise or believe
is incorrect, you can open a dispute. Disputes are handled by your card-
issuing bank in coordination with Visa.

## Before you call

Have ready:

- The transaction date and amount.
- The merchant name as shown on the statement.
- The reason for the dispute (unauthorised, duplicate, service not
  received, or service not as described).

## Steps

1. Contact your issuing bank using the customer-service number on the
   back of your card.
2. The bank will verify your identity and may ask you to confirm recent
   transactions.
3. If the case proceeds, the bank opens a chargeback with the merchant.
   You will receive a provisional credit while the case is investigated.
4. Investigations typically conclude within 30-60 days.

Fraudulent unauthorised transactions should be reported as quickly as
possible — most banks limit cardholder liability if reported promptly.
