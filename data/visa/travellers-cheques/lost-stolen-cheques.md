# Lost or stolen Visa Traveller's Cheques

If your Visa Traveller's Cheques are lost or stolen, contact the
**issuer** of the cheques as soon as possible. Visa itself does not
issue cheques directly — they are sold and refunded by partner banks.

## Common issuer contacts

- **Citicorp Diners Club**: Freephone 1-800-645-6556 or collect
  1-813-623-1709, Monday–Friday 6:30 am – 2:30 pm EST. Automated
  verification of cheque serial numbers is available 24/7 in English
  and Spanish. Calls placed outside business hours are recorded and
  returned the next business day.

## Have the following ready before you call

- Cheque serial numbers (from your purchase agreement / counterfoil).
- Date and place of purchase.
- How and when the cheques were lost or stolen.
- Name of the issuer.

## What to expect

Refunds can typically be arranged within 24 hours of the report, subject
to the cheque issuer's terms and conditions. Also notify the local
police; many issuers require a police report number to finalize the
refund.

## If you can't reach the issuer

Use Visa's Traveller's-Cheque contact form on visa.co.in to request
help. Provide as much detail as you can about the cheques and where you
are.
