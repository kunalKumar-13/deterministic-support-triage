# Deleting your DevPlatform account

To request deletion of your DevPlatform Community account:

1. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
2. Open **Account Settings > Privacy & data**.
3. Choose **Delete account** and follow the confirmation flow.

## Accounts created via Google / SSO login

If you signed up with Google or another single-sign-on provider, you do
not have a separate DevPlatform password — that is expected. Use the
**Continue with Google** button at the sign-in page to access the
account, then follow the deletion flow above.

## What gets deleted

- Profile information, badges, and solved problems on Community.
- Any assessment attempts you submitted as a candidate.
- Workspace seats you held under DevPlatform for Work (separately, your
  workspace admin must remove you from any active workspace before the
  account can be fully closed).

If your account is part of a paid workspace, contact your workspace
admin first; some deletion paths require admin acknowledgement.
