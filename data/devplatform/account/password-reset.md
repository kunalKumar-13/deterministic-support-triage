# Resetting your DevPlatform password

If you cannot sign in to your DevPlatform workspace, you can reset your
password from the sign-in page.

1. Open the sign-in page and choose **Forgot password?**.
2. Enter the email address associated with your DevPlatform account.
3. Check that inbox for a reset link from `noreply@devplatform.com`. The
   link is valid for 30 minutes.
4. Open the link and set a new password.

If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes:

- Check your spam folder.
- Ask your workspace admin to confirm your email is correctly listed.
- If your account uses single sign-on (SSO), password reset is handled by
  your identity provider, not DevPlatform.

Password resets do not change any of your test results, candidate data, or
workspace settings.
