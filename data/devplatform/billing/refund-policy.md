# DevPlatform refund policy

Refunds for workspace subscriptions follow these rules:

- **New subscriptions**: Eligible for a full refund within 14 days of the
  initial charge, provided the workspace has not been used to send more
  than five test invites.
- **Renewals**: Pro-rated refunds are available within 7 days of the
  renewal charge.
- **Add-on seats**: Refundable only for the current billing period and
  only for the unused portion (calculated daily).

To request a refund:

1. Contact billing support with the original invoice number.
2. The billing team will verify the invoice and the requesting user's
   identity before issuing the refund.
3. Refunds are issued to the original payment method and may take 5-10
   business days to appear.

Refunds for fraudulent charges are handled separately by the trust and
safety team; do not request these through the normal refund flow.
