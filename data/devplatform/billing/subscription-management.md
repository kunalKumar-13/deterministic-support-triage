# Managing your DevPlatform workspace subscription

Workspace owners can manage seats, plan tier, and billing settings.

## Pausing a subscription

You can pause a workspace subscription when you have temporarily
stopped hiring. While paused:

- Existing candidates retain access to in-flight tests.
- You cannot send new invites.
- Billing is suspended for the pause window.

To pause, open **Settings > Billing > Pause subscription** and choose a
pause length (1, 3, or 6 months). At the end of the window the
subscription resumes automatically.

If you need a custom pause duration or an immediate cancellation
instead, contact billing support with your workspace ID.

## Cancellation vs pause

- **Pause** is reversible and keeps your data live.
- **Cancellation** ends the subscription at the current period end.
  Your data is retained for 90 days, after which it is purged unless
  you renew.

## Educational / nonprofit pricing

Educational institutions and nonprofits may qualify for discounted
plans. Submit an application through **Settings > Billing > Educational
pricing**. Approval typically takes 5–7 business days.
